## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Location

Your data is stored using Supabase, which uses cloud infrastructure with data centers in the United States and other regions. Your specific data location may vary depending on Supabase's infrastructure. By using our Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection rules.

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data is encrypted in our databases
- **Authentication:** JWT-based token authentication with secure session management
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control and least-privilege principles
- **Regular Monitoring:** Continuous monitoring for suspicious activities and security threats
- **Secure File Storage:** Resume and job description files are stored in Supabase's secure storage with access controls

### 5.3 Data Retention

- **User Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion
- **Resume and Job Description Files:** Retained as long as you have analyses using them, and automatically cleaned up 24 hours after the last related analysis
- **Analysis Results:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Login/Session Data:** Retained for security and audit purposes for up to 90 days
- **Waitlist Data:** Retained until you convert to a paid user or request deletion

### 5.4 Limitation of Liability

While we implement appropriate security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. You use our Service at your own risk.

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 6.1 Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

**Session Cookies:**
- Maintain your login state while you browse the application
- Expire when you close your browser

**Persistent Cookies:**
- Remember your preferences and settings across sessions
- Can remain on your device for extended periods

**Authentication Cookies:**
- Store JWT tokens for secure authentication
- Managed through LocalStorage by our Supabase integration

### 6.2 Purpose of Cookies

- Maintaining session state and keeping you logged in
- Storing user preferences
- Enhancing security through authentication tokens
- Analyzing usage patterns to improve the Service

### 6.3 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings:
- Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent
- If you disable cookies, some features of the Service may not function properly

### 6.4 Third-Party Analytics

We may use third-party analytics services to understand how users interact with our Service. These services may place their own cookies on your device. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

## 9. Residents of Specific Jurisdictions

### 9.1 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know:** You can request what personal information we collect and how it is used
- **Right to Delete:** You can request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** You can opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of your personal information (we do not sell your information)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@alignai.cv. We will respond within 45 days.

### 9.2 European Union and UK Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or UK, your personal information is processed under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Your rights include:

- **Right of Access:** You can request a copy of your personal information
- **Right to Rectification:** You can correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Right to Erasure:** You can request deletion of your information ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** You can request limitation of how your information is used
- **Right to Data Portability:** You can request your information in a portable format
- **Right to Object:** You can object to certain types of processing
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint:** You can file a complaint with your local data protection authority

Our legal basis for processing your information includes: contract performance, legal compliance, and legitimate interests. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@alignai.cv.

### 9.3 Other Jurisdictions

We comply with applicable privacy laws in all jurisdictions where we operate. If your jurisdiction provides additional privacy rights, please contact us to learn more.
