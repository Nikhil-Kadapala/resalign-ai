## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website or use an application. Cookies contain information that can be retrieved by the website or application that created them.

### 2.1 Types of Cookies

**Based on Duration:**
- **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Cookies that remain on your device for a set period until they expire or are manually deleted

**Based on Source:**
- **First-Party Cookies:** Cookies set directly by ResAlign AI
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Cookies set by third-party services integrated with our Service

**Based on Purpose:**
- **Strictly Necessary Cookies:** Required for core functionality
- **Functional Cookies:** Remember your preferences and settings
- **Analytics Cookies:** Track usage and performance
- **Marketing Cookies:** Used for targeted advertising (we do not use these)

## 3. Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Service to function properly. They enable you to log in, maintain your session, and use core features.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| `sb-auth-token` | Supabase authentication JWT token | Session or 24 hours | First-Party |
| `sb-session-id` | Session identifier for tracking authenticated user | Session | First-Party |
| `_session_id` | Session tracking and CSRF protection | Session | First-Party |

**Function:** These cookies are required for:
- User authentication and login
- Maintaining secure sessions
- Protecting against cross-site request forgery (CSRF)
- Preventing unauthorized access to your account

**Legal Basis:** These cookies are necessary for contract performance (providing the Service as agreed in our Terms of Service).

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences, settings, and choices to enhance your experience.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| `user_preferences` | UI preferences (theme, language, layout) | 1 year | First-Party |
| `last_tab` | Remember your last active tab/section | Session | First-Party |

**Function:** These cookies store:
- Language and localization preferences
- UI theme preferences (if applicable)
- User interface state and navigation history

**Legal Basis:** These cookies are used based on your consent and legitimate interests in providing a personalized experience.

### 3.3 LocalStorage Data

While not technically cookies, we use browser LocalStorage for similar purposes:

| Storage Key | Purpose | Persistence |
|---|---|---|
| `sb-session` | Supabase session persistence (stored in localStorage by our SDK) | Until cleared |
| `sb-user` | Cached user information | Until cleared |
| `app-preferences` | Application preferences | Until cleared |

**Function:** LocalStorage is used by Supabase SDK to:
- Persist authentication sessions across browser restarts
- Store user authentication tokens securely
- Cache user information for faster loading

## 8. Managing Your Cookies

### 8.1 Browser Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

**Access Cookie Settings:**
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data

**Options Available:**
- Accept all cookies
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Delete cookies automatically on exit

### 8.2 Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, please note that:
- The Service may not function properly
- You may not be able to log in or stay logged in
- Some features may become unavailable
- You may need to re-authenticate frequently

### 8.3 Third-Party Opt-Out

You can opt out of specific third-party cookies:

**Google Analytics Opt-Out:**
- Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on
- Visit: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

**Supabase:**
- Manage your preferences in your account settings
- Review Supabase privacy settings at: https://supabase.com/privacy

### 8.4 "Do Not Track" Signals

Some browsers support "Do Not Track" (DNT) signals. While we respect your privacy, we do not currently respond to DNT signals. However, you can manage cookies through the methods described above.

## 15. Definitions
